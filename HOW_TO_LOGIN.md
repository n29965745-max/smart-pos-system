# How to Login to Your Shop - Simple Guide

## The Confusion

You were visiting `/s/nylawigs` and seeing Nyla Wigs branding. This is **correct** - that's Nyla Wigs' public storefront for their customers.

## The Solution

**You don't need to visit any shop page to access the admin dashboard!**

## Direct Login (Recommended)

### Step 1: Go to the Universal Login Page
```
https://smart-pos-system-peach.vercel.app/login
```

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials
- Email: your email
- Password: your password

### Step 3: Click "Sign In"

### Step 4: You're In!
You'll be redirected to `/dashboard-pro` where you can manage YOUR shop (Prime Tech Electronics).

## That's It!

No need to visit `/s/nylawigs` or any other shop page. Just bookmark the login page and use it directly.

---

## What Each URL Does

| URL | What It Is | Who Uses It |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| `/login` | **Universal login** | Shop owners (YOU) |
| `/dashboard-pro` | **Admin dashboard** | Shop owners (YOU) |
| `/s/nylawigs` | Nyla Wigs public page | Nyla Wigs customers |
| `/s/primetech` | Prime Tech public page | Prime Tech customers |

## Key Point

**Public shop pages (`/s/[slug]`) are for CUSTOMERS, not for admin access.**

To manage your shop, always use:
1. `/login` to sign in
2. `/dashboard-pro` to manage your shop
