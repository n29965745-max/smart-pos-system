## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document outlines a comprehensive strategy to transform your online shop into a world-class ecommerce marketplace, inspired by AliExpress, Temu, Amazon, and Shopee, while maintaining your unique brand identity and market positioning.

**Goal:** Create a high-conversion, mobile-first marketplace that exceeds AliExpress in user experience, trust, and engagement.

---

## STRATEGIC POSITIONING

### Your Competitive Advantage
- **Local Market Focus:** Faster delivery, local payment methods, local language
- **Trust-First Approach:** Better buyer protection than AliExpress
- **Simplified UX:** Less clutter, faster checkout, clearer navigation
- **Integrated POS:** Unique advantage - online + offline unified system
- **Better Seller Support:** Easier onboarding, better tools, lower fees

### Target Metrics (6 Months)
- Conversion Rate: 3.5% → 8%
- Cart Abandonment: 70% → 35%
- Average Order Value: +45%
- Repeat Purchase Rate: 15% → 40%
- Session Duration: 2min → 8min
- Mobile Conversion: 2% → 6%
