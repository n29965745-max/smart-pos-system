## 🆕 What's New

### 1. **Wishlist System** ❤️
- Heart icon in header with count badge
- Add products to wishlist from anywhere
- Wishlist modal showing all saved items
- Move wishlist items directly to cart

**How to use:**
- Click the heart icon on product cards (appears on hover)
- Click "Add to Wishlist" in product details
- View your wishlist by clicking the heart icon in header

Sort products by:

- Name: A to Z

**How to use:**
- Use the dropdown menu above the product grid
- Sorting works with all filters and search

### 3. **View Modes** 👁️
Switch between:
- **Grid View** - Compact cards (6 columns on desktop)

**How to use:**
- Click the grid/list icons above the product grid
- View mode persists while browsing
