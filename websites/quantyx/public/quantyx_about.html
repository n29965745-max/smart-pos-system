Our Story

From Vision to Reality

QuantyX Digital Technologies was founded by Bruno Murimi Wachira with a singular vision: to democratize access to world-class digital solutions. What started as a passion project has evolved into a powerhouse of innovation and excellence.

Bruno's journey began in the heart of Kenya's tech ecosystem, where he witnessed countless businesses struggling to navigate the digital transformation. Recognizing this gap, he assembled a team of brilliant minds—developers, designers, strategists, and visionaries—all united by a common purpose: to empower businesses through technology.

Today, QuantyX stands as a beacon of digital excellence, having transformed hundreds of businesses across multiple industries. We've grown from a small startup to a full-service digital agency, yet we've never lost sight of what matters most: delivering exceptional results for our clients.

👨‍💼 Founder & CEO

Bruno Murimi Wachira - Visionary leader with 10+ years of experience in digital transformation, software development, and business strategy. Bruno's mission is to make cutting-edge digital solutions accessible to businesses of all sizes.
